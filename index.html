Events

Open Mic

Our most popular event, the Open Mic Night is open to all musicians and performing artists to showcase their talent. This event is offered about once a month in both the Fall and Spring semester

Battle of the Bands

Lehigh student bands have the chance to compete in our battle of the bands for prizes and the opportunity to have a slot in the LVAIC Battle of the Bands, also hosted by Music Box

Frattle of the Bands

Music Box's annual philanthropic event gives a chance for on-campus fraternities to form bands and perform infront of a live to active crowd. All money made for the event is donated to the Boys and Girls Club of Bethlehem.

About us

We are a young but rapidly growing and ambitious organization that cooperates with Lehigh After Dark, University Productions, and WLVR (Lehigh's premier radio station) to bring to campus the best live music atmosphere possible.

    The Music Box's goals are described as follows:
  • To act as a gathering place and network for Lehigh musicians where they can form bands, learn from each other's talents, and appreciate their different styles of playing and musical interstest.
  • To provide student musicians with rehearsal space and gear (drum set, P.A. system, amps, mics, etc.) that are not provided by Lehigh.
  • To organize and host events that allow student musicians to showcase their talents and gain experience with live performance. We currently host monthly open mic nights, the Lehigh Battle of the Bands series, acoustic showcases on the front lawn, and charity concerts such as the newly created Frattle of the Bands.
  • To hold a database and connections with professional musicians, managers, venues, studios, labels, booking agents, and other experts within the music industry to provide student bands and musicians with opportunities to perform and network outside of the Lehigh community. We promote ambitious goals while providing the resources necessary to achieve those goals.

In addition to this, we will also host musician's workshops as well as supply all other organizations on campus with our repertoire of musical acts to provide live music entertainment when needed. This means that solo musicians and musical groups joining our organization will obtain guaranteed experience in performing in front of a wide array of diverse audiences.
