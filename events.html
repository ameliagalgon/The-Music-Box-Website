Our Events

Music Box provides many events for students to perform in such as our open mics and our Battle of the Bands. There is something new every semester, so don't forget to check our calendar to stay updated on our events

Open Mic Night

Our most popular event, the Open Mic Night is open to all musicians and performing artists to showcase their talent. This event is offered about once a month in both the Fall and Spring semester

Battle of the Bands

Lehigh student bands have the chance to compete in our battle of the bands for prizes and the opportunity to have a slot in the LVAIC Battle of the Bands, also hosted by Music Box

Frattle of the Bands

Music Box's annual philanthropic event gives a chance for on-campus fraternities to form bands and perform infront of a live to active crowd. All money made for the event is donated to the Boys and Girls Club of Bethlehem.
